General Facts

Some General Facts that could be kept in mind for healthy lifestyle

Maintain Proper Posture

Maintaining good posture is essential for reducing strain on the body. Sit and stand with your back straight, shoulders relaxed, and feet flat on the floor. Avoid slouching or hunching over.

Adjust Your Workspace

Adjust your chair height so that your feet are flat on the floor and your knees are at a 90-degree angle. Position your monitor at eye level and ensure your keyboard and mouse are within comfortable reach.

Take Regular Breaks

Take frequent breaks from prolonged sitting or repetitive tasks. Stand up, stretch, and move around to reduce muscle fatigue and improve circulation. Consider using tools like standing desks or ergonomic chairs that encourage movement.

Screen at eye level

Always keep the computer screen at the correct eye level to prevent strain in eyes and neck. You can use a monitor for this purpose.

Drink Water

Drink water once in every half or one hour. It is important to always stay hydrated.

20 20 20 Rule

While using a screen, take a break after every 20 minutes to look 20 feet away for 20 seconds. This will protect our eyes.
