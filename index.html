ABOUT ErgoTracker

Ergonomics is a science that deals with the individual, his environment and his activity at work or at home. It involves the study of postures, blood circulation aspects, back problems and issues related to an individual's physical limitations. In simple words it helps people to do their work more efficiently and with fewer chances of injury and illness. By using Ergonomic principles one can reduce stresses and strains on the body which ultimately helps health and safety of the individual.

These days, multiple factors are putting people at risk for aches and pains which are related to improper ergonomics which is leading to more and more people suffer from repetitive strain injuries (RSI). It is proven that Ergonomics can help people to work safely and reduce chances of physical injury.

We bring to you ways in which you can train yourself for the same.
